# Hod Project - Simple Budget Tracker

A streamlined budget-tracking application designed to help users manage their finances with clarity, control, and simplicity. The app allows users to set a budget, record expenses in real time, and automatically track the remaining balance.

## Features

### Core Functionality
- **Budget Setup & Updates** - Define initial budget amount in RWF and make flexible updates
- **Real-Time Expense Tracking** - Log purchases with description and amount in RWF
- **Automatic Balance Calculation** - Real-time subtraction from total budget
- **Budget Completion Handling** - Smart notifications when budget is fully used
- **Expense History** - Complete visibility of all transactions with dates
- **Shared View Access**
Users can share their budget dashboard with others.
Shared users can:
  - Track expenses
  - Add new expenses
  - View remaining balance
  - Access transaction history
Supports collaborative budget tracking while protecting budget settings.
- **Clean Dashboard** - Clear view of budget, spent, and remaining amounts
- **Progress Tracking** - Visual progress bar with color-coded warnings
- **Local Storage** - Data persists between sessions
- **Responsive Design** - Works perfectly on desktop and mobile devices
- **No-Nonsense Interface** - Focused on speed and simplicity

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- No additional software required

### Installation
1. Download or clone the project files
2. Open `index.html` in your web browser
3. Start tracking your budget immediately!

### Quick Start
1. **Set Budget**: Click "Edit" next to Total Budget to set your initial amount
2. **Add Expenses**: Enter description and amount, then click "Add Expense"
3. **View Progress**: Monitor your spending through the progress bar and remaining balance
4. **Share Budget**: Click "Share" to generate a view-only link for others

## Usage Guide

### Setting Your Budget
- Click the "Edit" button next to your total budget
- Enter a new budget amount or add funds to existing budget
- Changes take effect immediately

### Adding Expenses
- Enter a description (e.g., "Coffee at Starbucks")
- Enter the amount spent
- Click "Add Expense" - the amount is automatically deducted from your budget

### Budget Completion
When your budget is fully used:
- **Add More Funds** - Increase your budget amount
- **Reset Budget** - Start fresh with a new budget
- **Continue Tracking** - Keep tracking even with negative balance

### Sharing Your Budget
- Click "Share" to generate a unique link
- Share the link with family, friends, or team members
- Viewers can see your budget and expenses but cannot make changes

## Data Management

### Local Storage
- All data is stored locally in your browser
- No accounts or cloud storage required
- Data persists between browser sessions

### Data Export
- Use the share feature to create a backup link
- Bookmark the share link for future reference

### Data Reset
- **Clear History** - Remove all expense records while keeping budget
- **Reset All** - Complete reset of budget and expenses
- **Warning**: These actions cannot be undone

## Design Philosophy

### "No-Noise Budgeting"
Hod Project follows these principles:
- **Speed First** - Complete common tasks in under 3 seconds
- **Clarity Over Features** - Essential information prominently displayed
- **Minimal Cognitive Load** - Clean, distraction-free interface
- **Instant Gratification** - Immediate feedback for all actions

### Differentiation
Unlike complex apps like Mint or YNAB:
- No accounts or setup required
- No unnecessary categories or tags
- No ads or premium features
- Focus on the core job: track your budget effectively
